BST TEAM

The BST project was born out of the fierceness of four blockchain enthusiasts . The arrival of smart contracts on blockchain, initiated by the Ethereum network and then followed by others, has created in recent years a niche for nefarious and malicious people more commonly known as "SCAM" So we decided to take the bull by the horns and launch into the fight against them! Our developpement team purposes are to make blockchain Safer for users by providing multiple services : - An ERC-20 TOKEN which is the main currency of our ecosystem . ( details in tokenomics faq). - A Contract Translator that provides user clear informations about the scanned contract ( see more details in Script Tranlator faq ). LET'S MAKE BLOCKCHAIN SAFER TOGETHER I decided to start my own project which is BST coin V1 on the Binance Smart Chain. This project is currently in the hands of the community as a "meme coin" and we have launched with the development team the second version of BST Coin on ERC-20 network and which will focus on strengthening the security through different means detailed in the "Whitepaper" of the project. The development of the BST Token and its Ecosystem will be my main occupations for the coming years, in order to bring to the Blockchain, a security and a wider use through various awareness programs."
